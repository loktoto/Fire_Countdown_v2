# Product

## Register

product

## Users

People actively planning for financial independence who need to record cash flow quickly, understand the consequence of each decision, and keep their assets and assumptions current without surrendering control of their data.

## Product Purpose

Fire Countdown turns local financial records into a deterministic, understandable FIRE plan. The primary jobs are fast daily logging, reviewing and correcting history, maintaining assets and goals, comparing projection scenarios, and seeing a clear countdown to financial independence.

## Brand Personality

Calm, candid, and quietly optimistic. The interface should feel like a trusted personal instrument: warm enough for daily use, precise enough for financial decisions, and distinctive without becoming decorative.

## Anti-references

Avoid neon crypto dashboards, generic fintech navy-and-gold styling, repetitive rounded-card grids, decorative glass effects, over-animated metrics, and interfaces that look assembled from an AI UI prompt. Do not gamify serious financial information or hide assumptions behind visual spectacle.

## Design Principles

1. Put the user's next financial decision before decoration.
2. Give each screen a distinct job and hierarchy while keeping controls familiar.
3. Show calculations, assumptions, provenance, and edit affordances plainly.
4. Use color for state and action, not as ambient decoration.
5. Reward continued use with clarity, responsiveness, and small moments of warmth.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Maintain clear contrast, 44-point touch targets, meaningful labels and states, reduced-motion support, scalable text, and layouts that remain understandable without relying on color alone. English and Traditional Chinese are equal product surfaces.
